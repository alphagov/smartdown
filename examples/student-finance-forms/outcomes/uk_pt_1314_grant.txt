# Student finance – application form

To apply for a Fee Grant and Course Grant use form PTGC (or PTGN if you're applying for student finance for the first time).

Academic year | Form
- | -
2013 to 2014 | [PTGC - form (PDF, 400KB)](http://www.sfengland.slc.co.uk/media/635660/ptgc_1314_d.pdf)
2013 to 2014 | [PTGC - guidance notes (PDF, 100KB)](http://www.sfengland.slc.co.uk/media/640247/ptgc_notes_1314_d.pdf)
2013 to 2014 | [PTGN - form (PDF, 400KB)](http://www.sfengland.slc.co.uk/media/635663/ptgn_1314_d.pdf) – if you’re applying for the first time
2013 to 2014 | [PTGN - guidance notes (PDF, 100KB)](http://www.sfengland.slc.co.uk/media/639169/sfe_ptgn_notes_1314_d.pdf)

^You can apply up to 9 months after the start of your course.^

##Additional forms

You may need to include additional information on the following forms.

Form | When to use the form
- | -
[CO2 - change of circumstances (PDF, 100KB)](http://www.sfengland.slc.co.uk/media/593367/sfe_co2_form_1314_d.pdf) | Your course, university or contact details change
[PTCI2 - income confirmation (PDF, 150KB)](http://www.sfengland.slc.co.uk/media/635654/ptci2_1314_d.pdf) | You don't have a P60, P11D or payslips to prove your income
[CB1 - benefits confirmation (PDF, 200KB)](http://www.sfengland.slc.co.uk/media/635657/ptcb1_1314_d.pdf) | You or your partner get certain benefits (check the form for details)
